Steps to becoming a Mentee

  • 1
    Sign up as a Mentee here at Prospela
    Complete our 5-min sign-up form, which will help us match you with real employees
  • 2
    Complete your online training
    It takes just 10 min and you'll learn the fundamentals of mentoring, how to build your professional network and make the most of your new connections!
  • 3
    Get introduced to your E-Mentor!
    You'll receive your E-Mentor's profile. We'll prep you and help you nail your introduction to get started!
  • 4
    Enjoy ongoing support & advice from Prospela
    It's not just your E-Mentor who'll be with you along the way. The Prospela team are on-hand to support you as you progress!
