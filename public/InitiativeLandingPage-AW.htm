Connecting women in the VFX & Animation Industry to the next generation of talent

We are pleased to announce that Animated Women UK have partnered with Disney UK & Ireland to create a mentorship programme for women in the Animation and VFX industries. This online initiative brings together women currently working within these industries and connects them with aspiring talent to promote the growth of the next generation of women.

Steps to becoming a Mentee

  • 1
    Sign up as a Mentee here at Prospela
    Complete our 5-min sign-up form, which will help us match you with real employees
  • 2
    Complete your online training
    It takes just 10 min and you'll learn the fundamentals of mentoring, how to build your professional network and make the most your new connections!
  • 3
    Get introduced to your E-Mentor!
    You'll receive your E-Mentor's profile. We'll prep you and help you nail your introduction to get started!
  • 4
    Enjoy ongoing support & advice from Prospela
    It's not just your E-Mentor who'll be with you along the way. The Prospela team are on-hand to support you as you progress!
