Why Join?

  • As an E-Mentor

Help garner a more inclusive and diverse arena for the gaming industry

We'll connect you with Mentees from all walks of life, aspiring to break into, and progress in, your industry

Mentor students online anytime, anywhere

With no set time or travel commitments, you can interact on your mobile or computer from wherever you like

Get trained on how to help students make informed career decisions

Learn how to utilise your invaluable experience in the workplace to help underserved students get off on the right foot

Enjoy ongoing support and guidance whenever you need it

The Prospela team (and your fellow VGA Ambassadors!) are on hand to personally support you throughout your e-mentoring experience.

Steps to becoming an E-Mentor

  • 1
    Complete the E-Mentor application form here at Prospela
    Complete our 5-min sign-up form, which will help us match you with your Mentee(s)
  • 2
    Complete your online training
    It takes just 10 min and will help you better understand your role, how to manage your mentee(s), and the best approach to giving advice & feedback to young people from underserved backgrounds
  • 3
    Get introduced to your Mentee(s)!
    You'll receive your Mentee's profile. We'll prep you and help you introduce yourself
  • 4
    Enjoy ongoing support & advice from Prospela
    The Prospela team (and your fellow VGA Ambassadors!) are on-hand to support you as your mentoring relationships progress and whenever you need us!
